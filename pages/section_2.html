Curatorial Platforms

To get an understanding of the importance of online curatorial platforms, we might commence by observing the dynamic and vast nature of the internet. Millions of websites and web pages are constantly being created, updated, and deleted, and with the sheer volume of information available, it would be impossible for a single person to be aware of all the relevant information related to this field, or any other one for that matter. Online curatorial platforms emerge in the form of collections, carefully presenting us with a rich palette of webpages that explore contemporary uses of the browser. Through the global communities formed around a broadcasted voice in design, the synergistic nature of the relationship between the designer and the curatorial platform, is propelling interaction and graphic design into a new stage.

Template Hell: No Code Cornucopia

The rise of ready-made website platforms has made it easier than ever to create a website, but this convenience has come at a cost. The abundance of templates available has led to a phenomenon known as “template hell”, where websites all begin to look the same and lack originality. This not only affects businesses, but it also stifles creativity in the web design community. Boring and repetitive templates limit the imagination and vision of designers, forcing them to conform to pre-existing design patterns. This can result in a homogenized web, where creativity and uniqueness are lost. It is important to remember that a website is more than just a platform for information; it is a canvas for creativity and self-expression. On the other hand, independent web design studios offer a breath of fresh air in this homogenized web. These studios prioritize creativity and originality, producing websites that are not only functional but also visually stunning. They provide a space for designers to experiment, push boundaries, and showcase their talent. This not only benefits businesses, but it also inspires other designers and helps to cultivate a vibrant web design community. Furthermore, independent web design studios often curate web design, showcasing the best of the best and promoting the importance of creativity in web design. By doing so, they encourage designers to continue pushing the boundaries and strive for excellence in their craft. In conclusion, while ready-made templates may be quick and convenient, they often lack the creativity and originality that is vital for a successful and impactful website. Independent web design studios provide a much-needed alternative, promoting creativity and originality in the web design community. They offer a platform for designers to experiment, express their vision, and showcase their talent. In a world where the internet is an integral part of our daily lives, it is essential that we support and celebrate the creativity and originality of independent web design studios.
